Ink Against Cancer

"Re-Inking" Their Website

I worked with my three teammates — Ariel, Cassandra, and Mark — to redesign Ink Against Cancer's website. Our goal was to make the website user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing.

The Problem

User interviews revealed that it was difficult to locate the volunteer forms, that the navigation bar might not register to a user as a useful part of the website, and that there were some redundancies

Our Solution

My Responsibilities

Overcoming Obstacles

One of our teammates did not complete the tasks they agreed to work on, and they did this on a consistent basis. I met with my other two teammates, and we decided to work harder to ensure that our output was up to quality, escalate the issue to our instructors, and act as cordial as we could towards our friendly yet ineffective teammate.
