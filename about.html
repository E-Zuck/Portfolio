About

Elizabeth Zuck is a UX designer with a passion for learning and understanding others. Operating from Murray, Utah, she is a creative thinker with a sharp eye for detail, a flexible personality, and a unique perspective of the world.

Driven in part by a desire to excel, she aims to sharpen her skills of information architecture, UX copywriting, user research, and the many other tasks one can find in a UXer’s toolbelt. Elizabeth values communication and harmony in the workplace, quality over quantity, and the beauty that can be found in the world—beauty of all sorts, from nature to well-designed font families.

In her spare time, she can be found reading or spending time with her family, and she endeavors to become more proficient as an artist. Elizabeth has attended the University of Utah, and she has completed a bootcamp course sponsored by the University and EdX.
